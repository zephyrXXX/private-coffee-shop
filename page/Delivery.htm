ORDERING FOR A FRIEND?


Follow these steps to share an order.

Step 1

SEND TO FRIEND

Choose the lucky friend. Enter their address into the Uber Eats app and order their favorite items.

Step 2

PLACE ORDER

After placing an order on Uber Eats, click the share icon in the top right corner to send a delivery tracking link and a special message to your friend.

Step 3

TRACK COFFEE

Your friend can then follow the delivery through the tracking link sent.
